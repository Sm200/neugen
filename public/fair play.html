Fair Play

We, at Zone11, as a Fantasy Gaming platform, take it as our prior responsibility to ensure the culture of Fair Play within the Fantasy arena. Throughout the game flow of our application, we are fully aligned to the Supreme Court’s precision of ‘game of skill’ that includes a predominance of knowledge, training, attention & experience of the player. We do not encourage acts of gambling, betting, or any acts of cheating thereby making fantasy sports a legal and ethical way of enjoying what you love!

We provide and promote:

EQUAL OPPORTUNITY FOR ALL

All users when selecting a fantasy team for any match are provided with the same budget of 100 credits. All users have access to the complete database of players and are bound by the same rules while picking the composition of their respective teams, such as the maximum/minimum limits on the number of batsmen, bowlers, wicket-keeper and all-rounders and limits placed on the number of players that can be picked from a particular real life team.

FIXED PLAYER CREDITS

The credits required to pick a player are locked for each particular contest and match. The price for each player does not fluctuate based on the performance of the player or their real life team during the particular contest or the match.

LEGALITY

Zone11 is a skill based platform and is offered in accordance with Indian laws. To check the Legality visit the Zone11 site.

COMMUNICATION

We understand that being accessible to our users builds trust and provides security. So, we've made it easier for any user to reach out to us through support@zone11.app so that their queries are addressed on a timely basis.

GENUINE, VERIFIED USERS

We strive to ensure that we only have genuine users playing on Zone11.. To facilitate this, we have put in place strict policies and protocols to check any violation of Zone11 FairPlay guidelines and criteria. Before withdrawing their winnings, the Users are required to verify their accounts & furnish their valid government authorized ID Proof - PAN Card & bank account details. These details and documents are collected and verified with the respective authorities.

SAFE & SECURE PAYMENTS

We work with trusted third party payment gateways with 3-D secure authentication to ensure safety and security of all financial transactions. We do not store your credit/debit card details with us.You can rest assured for safe and secure transactions.

PROTECT USER INFO

Our various users’ trust us with their personal documents like PAN Cards and Bank details. We value & uphold that trust in high esteem. The information shared with us is only used for the user verification purposes and is never disclosed to any outside parties.

About Zone11

Zone11 is a digital fantasy Cricket platform designed with an aim to let fans play fantasy cricket and enjoy being part of the "real game".

It helps Indian Cricket fans showcase their knowledge and put to use their skills by actively engaging with the sport they love.

Fans get a hands-on experience of creating their own team of real players from the upcoming matches. They thereby,  score points based on the players' on-field performances and win rewards by competing with other such fans.
